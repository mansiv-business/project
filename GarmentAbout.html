Apparel Textile

At Apparel Textile, we help global retailers and brands source large volumes of clothing from trusted garment factories in Bangladesh. Our goal is simple: to make your sourcing process easier, faster, and more reliable.

With years of experience and strong connections in the local industry, we make sure you get high-quality products, competitive prices, and on-time delivery — every time.

MISSION

Our mission is to help retailers grow by giving them direct access to Bangladesh’s trusted garment factories — delivering high-quality apparel at affordable prices, with full reliability from order to delivery

Frequently asked questions:

1. Why should we source from Bangladesh?

Bangladesh offers high-quality clothing at competitive prices. It’s one of the world’s top garment exporters, and we make the process safe and reliable for you.




2. How do you ensure supplier reliability?

We only work with trusted factories. Each one is checked for quality and compliance before we start production.




3. Can you handle private label or custom designs?

Yes, we can produce clothing under your own brand, from design to packaging.




4. Do you work with small businesses?

We mainly focus on big retailers, but small-to-medium bulk orders are also possible.




5. How do I get started?

Just contact us with your requirements. We’ll guide you through the process step by step.

Have questions or want to start your order? Contact us and we’ll make sourcing from Bangladesh simple and reliable for you.
