From Factory to Store: Quality Clothing, Simplified

We help retailers source high-quality apparel from Bangladesh, handling every step so your orders arrive on time, at the right quality, and with full support.

Services We Provide:

1.Supplier Sourcing

We connect you with trusted factories in Bangladesh that meet quality, compliance, and capacity standards.


Focus: mainly large retailers, though we also accept small-to-medium bulk orders.





2.Quality Assurance

Our team ensures every product goes through strict quality checks, so you receive only what meets international standards.


Every order is inspected before shipping to ensure consistency and reliability.





3.Private Label & Custom Production

We help retailers create their own branded clothing lines, from fabric selection to packaging, tailored to your requirements.


Samples available upon request.





4.Logistics & Delivery

We manage shipping and documentation so your orders arrive safely and on time, within 2–4 months.


Delivery usually takes 2–4 months, depending on order size and customization.

Ready to start your order? Contact us and we’ll guide you through every step.
